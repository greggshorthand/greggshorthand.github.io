Anniversary Reading Material

The Story of John Robert Gregg

A short biography of Gregg, told in his words, and written in shorthand over the course of three issues of the Gregg Writer.

Aesop’s Fables

A collection of the ancient fables of Aesop, written in shorthand.

A Christmas Carol, by Charles Dickens

A slightly abridged telling of Dickens's classic, written in shorthand.

The Diamond Necklace, by Guy de Maupassant

A translation of the famous short story, La Parure, into English, and written in shorthand.

Do You Speak Headlinese?

A short article from the Gregg Writer about how language has changed as a result of newspapers.

The Great Stone Face, by Nathaniel Hawthorne

The classic short story, written in shorthand.

Greek vs. Shorthand

An article lauding the worth of shorthand as an art for the edification of the mind and culture, comparable to (and more useful than) the classic languages Greek and Latin, in high school education. From the Gregg Writer.

God's Grandeur, by Gerard Manley Hopkins

The famous poem by Hopkins, written in humble penmanship by Andrew Owen.
