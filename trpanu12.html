Unit 12 Transcription Key

     110.  Reading and Dictation Practice
            Dear Sir: This letter is in answer to your recent communication
            regarding my long overdue bill for purchases.
            I wish to thank you for the friendly character of your remarks.

            Yes, the bill is correct in every particular and it is
            hard for me to explain my delay in
            paying it in full.

            You say that I may return
            any of the purchases that didn't suit me in every
            regard.  The truth of the matter is that I have liked
            everything I bought of your house.

            I shall send you a check in
            full within one week from date and I hope
            that will suit you.  After you have waited this long
            I suppose that one or two more days delay will not matter
            much.  If the check does not reach you by the end of
            next week, let me know by phone and I shall be glad
            to send you another without delay.  Yours truly,

            [157 words]
