92.  Frequent-Word Drill

 

     93.  The combination us is written without an angle at the beginning of words, or when it follows a downstroke or k, g, as in us, shoes, campus, cousin, etc.

     94. The -hook Modified.  To avoid an unnecessary angle, the -hook is turned under after n, m.  It is also turned under after k or g if followed by r or l.
