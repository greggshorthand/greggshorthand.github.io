Unit 35 Transcription Key

     245.  Reading and Dictation Practice
            Gentlemen:  It weakens our moral fiber when
            we lose faith.  That's why we don't want to lose faith
            in you, in your good company, and in your manner of
            doing business.

            Your past-due account—now more than
            three months old—is a matter of grave
            concern with us at this time.  The old year is slipping
            into the past, and we know you want to start the new year*
            right by having all past-due accounts paid.  We, too,
            are greatly interested in having our own books in
            proper balance for the year.

            The amount that you owe
            us is only $45.98.  Won't you mail us your check
            today for this small amount and dismiss the
            matter from your mind?

            We wish for your continued prosperity
            and happiness during the coming year, and thank you for all
            your past and future business. Yours very truly,

            [151 words]
