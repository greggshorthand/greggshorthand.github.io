Unit 24 Transcription Key

     190.  Reading and Dictation Practice
            Dear sir: Thank you for your order of January 15.  It will go
            out promptly.  We feel confident that you will find "Sunripe"
            oranges up to the high standard of the fruit you have been handling.
            Every precaution has been taken to select and pack the
            oranges carefully before shipping.  Please wire us if they then reach you
            on time and in good condition.

            We are sending you today
            some window cards and a number of small
            folders presenting recent facts about the quantity of fruit
            consumed in this country.  These folders also stress the known
            value of fruit in maintaining health and in addition
            give many recipes for tempting dishes.

            Give
            them freely to all who come into your store and
            they will unquestionably increase your fruit sales.

            Your orders will always receive our careful attention.  Will you not
            let us serve you again? Yours very truly,

            [149 words]
