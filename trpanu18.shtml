Unit 18 Transcription Key

     151.  Reading and Dictation Practice
            Dear sir: Tomorrow will be the beginning of our fiftieth year
            in business at Eighth and Broadway.  To mark this
            happy occasion we have printed a completely new catalogue
            of our merchandise, have advertised the event in all the newspapers,
            and in every way have tried to acquaint the general public
            with our many improvements and the quality and quantity
            of our stock.

            Nevertheless, the outcome of such endeavors
            is not satisfactory to us if our records do not show that our
            friends are pleased in every respect.  Won't you
            use the enclosed envelope to tell us about any difficulty
            that may be keeping you from using your charge account
            more freely?

            Please accept the enclosed souvenir with our
            complements, read the announcement of our special
            sale in honor of this event, and plan
            to come in and visit us tomorrow.  Yours very truly,

            [144 words]
