Unit 22 Transcription Key

     177.  Reading and Dictation Practice
            Dear sir: We hope you can be induced to take over for the autumn
            some of our finest costumes which are now being sold at
            greatly reduced prices.  We have one of the largest and finest
            assortments we have ever displayed and all of these costumes
            are representative of the latest fashions.  They are the products of
            one of the oldest houses and were designed by the most famous
            style artists.  Some are exact copies of imported
            garments; others are adapted to make them conform
            to the dress ideals of this country.

            We expect to put on a fashion
            display beginning next Tuesday.  Some of the best-known
            mannequins in the Estate will appear.  We hope you can attend.
            We suggest an early preview, as there is certain to be a great sale
            for these products and the supply will soon be exhausted.

            Entry is by card.  We are glad to send you a
            number of these for the use of your buyers and others. Very truly yours,

            [168 words]
