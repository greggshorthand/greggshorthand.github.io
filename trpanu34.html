Unit 34 Transcription Key

     240.  Reading and Dictation Practice
            Dear Sir:  Our records show that you have not sent any orders to us
            recently, and the absence of any business from you causes us to
            wonder.

            Our motto, "Every Customer is a Satisfied One,"
            is the result of quality and service.  If for some reason we have
            failed to attain the desired results, we are very
            anxious to learn the cause.

            We comb the
            potato country for the very best produce, and select
            only the best obtainable, taking the stock into our
            storerooms and sorting by the most modern methods.
            We are in a position to satisfy the most particular customer
            as to both prices and quality.

            May we request a
            reply, informing us if the absence of your usual orders is due to
            dissatisfaction with our prices or quality, or to reasons
            having no bearing on our goods?

            Whether you are in a position
            to order or not, your reply will be appreciated. Yours very truly,

            [157 words]
