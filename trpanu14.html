Unit 14 Transcription Key

     125.  Reading and Dictation Practice
            Dear sir: In consideration of my experience with your company I wish you
                would be kind enough
            at your first opportunity, to mail to me, at the above
            address, a letter that I can show you my present employer.

            I am being considered for a higher position than the one
            I am now filling, and this letter from you would be
            quite a help.

            Thank your very much.  Yours respectfully,

            [70 words]

            Dear Sir:  I am wondering if you have made any progress with your
            study of a plan for a better handling of
            incoming and outgoing mail.  I recall that
            various arrangements were presented for your consideration.  By now you
                surely have
            come across a system that it would be to our advantage to
            install.

            I hope that you will not object to my
            questions now and then regarding our office methods, as
            I am always pleased to receive your opinions, which I value
            highly because of your wide experience.  Yours truly,

            [91 words]
