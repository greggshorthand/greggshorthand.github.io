     134.  The -nt blend is an upward curve, corresponding in length to the sign for f; the -mt, -md blend is an upward curve, corresponding in length to v.  The n or m governs the length of the curve; the curve containing m naturally is longer.  At the beginning of words, short e and short i are omitted before these blends, as in entry, empty, induce, etc.
